How It Works

Step 1

Get Dropped Anywhere

You’re placed in a random real-world Street View location. Investigate the Clues, Pan around, inspect buildings, signs, terrain, and road markings to figure out where you are.

Step 2

Place Your Pin

Use Google Maps to zoom, pan, and compare regions, then place your pin exactly where you believe the Street View location is on the map.

Step 3

See Your Score

Compare your guess to the real location, see the distance, and earn points based on accuracy.

More Than a Game

Learn countries, regions, and cultural landmarks through real-world clues you analyze in every round.
Improve spatial and directional reasoning by comparing terrain, road systems, and geographic context before you guess.
Discover places you’ve never seen before and build stronger global intuition each time you play.

Location Dataset

Built on 10,000 real-world Street View locations

GeoGuesser rounds are generated from a curated pool of random locations to keep gameplay varied, replayable, and geographically diverse.

Canada, USA, Norway, France, Germany, Vietnam, and New Zealand are covered by Google Street View but are not represented in this dataset.

Dataset credit: Google Street View dataset
